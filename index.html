Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Trusted Name

Nestled in the heart of the town, Asian Thread House has stood the test of time for over 25 years. Built from the ground up by a single, determined individual, it started as a humble establishment and has grown into a trusted destination for both wholesale and retail customers.

Our shop is a vibrant trove of tailoring and craft essentials. Inside, neatly arranged shelves display a wide variety of threads in every shade imaginable, gleaming scissors, and all the small necessities that tailors rely on. We have a dedicated section for beautiful borders and dazzling kundan stones, perfect for adding flair to any garment or craft project.

From basic tailoring tools to intricate craft supplies, we cater to seasoned professionals and passionate hobbyists alike. It's not just a storeâ€”it's a legacy built on quality, trust, and years of dedicated service.
